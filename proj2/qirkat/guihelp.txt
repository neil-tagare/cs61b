Moves.
  To make a move, click on the piece you wish to move and then on each of the squares you wish to move in sequence.

Setting players
  The player type can be setted up in Options->Players. Default setting is white manual and black AI.

Starting and Restarting
  Selecting menu item Game->Start ends set-up mode and starts play.
  Selecting item Game->Clears the board the goes back to set-up mode
  Selecting item Game->Restart the board the goes back to playing mode
